Advice from experts

Learn every day

Best practices from top managers to boost communication efficiency

Focus on results Talk about value for the customer, rather than product features
Summarize agreements At the end of the call, clearly state the next steps and deadlines
Ask open-ended questions Instead of “Does this suit you?” ask “How do you envision solving this task?”
Empathy in conversation Acknowledge the customer's feelings: ”I understand how important this is to you”
Analyze your calls Listen to 2–3 of your conversations weekly and look for points of improvement
Active Listening Let the customer speak. Listen 70% of the time, speak 30%
